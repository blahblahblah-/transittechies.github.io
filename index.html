Transit Techies NYC

Bringing together the technologists who love transit, and the transit enthusiasts who hack!

We'll have speakers present transit-related projects. Presenters may be household hackers, data scientists, researchers, product developers, or you! All presentations will be technical and awesome.

Have an idea for a talk? Fill in our open call for speakers form (https://goo.gl/forms/yHRxsngEwGvJHQux2)!

Our group is inspired by the legendary DC Transportation Techies (https://www.meetup.com/Transportation-Techies/) run by Michael Schade (https://twitter.com/mvs202). We'd also like to thank Intersection (http://www.intersection.com) for generously hosting us at their beautiful Hudson Yards office.
